Illinois Badminton High School Foundation

Our Mission


The IBHSF, short for Illinois Badminton High School Foundation, is an Illinois high school organization dedicated to spreading badminton through supporting clubs and promoting the sport throughout schools. Our mission is twofold: make badminton readily available at high schools in Illinois, and make boys badminton an IHSA sponsored sport in the long run. We have decided to prioritize high schools in BIPOC communities receiving less funding (more in COVID-19 Implications). Currently, girls badminton is IHSA sponsored; however, boys badminton is not an IHSA sponsored sport due to there not being enough interest expressed by males in Illinois high schools. We will also attempt to help promote badminton as a sport, with means of sponsoring equipment, club organizers, and coaching.

COVID-19 Implications


As a result of COVID-19, our progress was significantly hindered. The original plan being to visit a local high school weekly quickly became unrealistic. We took, and are continuing to take, our time instead to focus on backend development, one being social media and two being this website. As we came to understand the severity of COVID-19 especially that it disproportionately affects BIPOC communities, we have decided to reignite our mission first partnering and working with local high schools that receive less funding and/or have been hurt by COVID-19.
